The Non-Linear Reality of the Pull Request Lifecycle

Developers lose productivity due to context switching from chasing reviewers, working on other tasks, and waiting for approvals.

How ManagerBot Brings Order to the Chaos

ManagerBot exposes the bottlenecks and provides engineering leaders with data to remove blockers and improve lead time.

The new way of doing things

Getting a pull request reviewed requires manual legwork; selecting a reviewer, following up multiple times to get anything done, and then an extensive back and forth. Not only is this a waste of time, but it’s a black hole, with little to no reporting on where your bottle necks are or how to improve things.
