The old way of doing things

Getting a pull request reviewed requires manual legwork; selecting a reviewer, following up multiple times to get anything done, and then an extensive back and forth. Not only is this a waste of time, but it's a black hole, with little to no reporting on where your bottle necks are or how to improve things.

The new way of doing things
  • managerbot reviews on push to decrease cycles allowing engineers to focus on substantial issues
  • managerbot notifies developers at the right time when there attention is required
  • managerbot provides actionable insights to improve your team's efficiency
Benefits for managers
  • Quickly identify bottlenecks in your CI/CD workflows
  • Make it much simpler for your engineers to adhere to defined process
  • Comprehensive end-to-end analytics
Benefits for developers
  • Massively reduce tedious legwork in getting PRs approved
  • Reduce context switching through elegant PR management
  • Direct notifications where you want them in Slack/Discord
  • Easily substantiate time spent on each PR
